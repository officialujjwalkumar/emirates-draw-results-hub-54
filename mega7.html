About MEGA7

MEGA7 is Emirates Draw's premier game offering players the chance to win the Middle East's largest Grand Prize of AED 100,000,000.

Players select 7 numbers from 1 to 35. Match all 7 numbers in any order to win the Grand Prize. There are also fantastic prizes for matching fewer numbers.

MEGA7 draws are held every Sunday at 9 PM. Don't miss your chance to become a millionaire!
